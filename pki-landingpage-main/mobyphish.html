MobyPhish

Enabling End-Users to Make Trust Decisions in the Context of HTTPS

About Us

We are a team of individuals from UCONN and Trinity College that created the extension "MobyPhish" to help the world get better at protecting themselves from phishing attacks.

The purpose of this study is to evaluate the effectiveness of a browser extension aimed at reducing the risk of users falling victim to phishing attacks. Our goal is to refine the extension based on the feedback and data obtained, ultimately developing an improved version for broader public release. The study will also provide valuable insights into the intersection of usability, privacy, and security.

How to Participate!

We are inviting undergraduate and graduate students from all disciplines to participate in our study!

The study will involve installing a browser extension and visiting a set of websites. The entire process should take approximately 30 minutes. For the short-term PKI study, you will receive a $20 Clincard, which acts as a debit card!
