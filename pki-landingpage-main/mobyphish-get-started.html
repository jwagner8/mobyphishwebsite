Get Started: MobyPhish Usability Study

Welcome to the MobyPhish usability study! As part of this research project, we’ve developed the browser extension “MobyPhish” to enhance your cybersecurity awareness and skills, specifically related to Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) vulnerabilities.

Start by watching Part 1 of the presentation. This will lead you to the consent form and pre-study survey.

After completing the survey, return to watch Part 2 of the presentation.

Perform tasks both with and without the MobyPhish extension. These tasks will evaluate its effectiveness against phishing attacks. Take note of your experiences during the tasks.

Finish by watching Part 3 of the presentation. This will lead you to the post-study survey.

Return to Part 3 of the presentation for thank you message.
